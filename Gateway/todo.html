Things To Do In Gatlinburg

Ripley's Aquarium of the Smokies

Ripley's Aquarium was named best Aquarium in North America by USA Today 10Best.com! Spectacular underwater tunnel with 12-foot sharks, giant sea turtle and thousands of exotic sea creatures! Playful penguins, dive/feeding shows, touch stingrays, horseshoe crabs, jellyfish and more!

Starting at $39

Suitable for all ages

Old Smoky Distillery

Visit Tennessee’s first legal moonshine distillery! A mason jar of Ole Smoky Moonshine is the finest souvenir in the Smokies. Sample a centuries-old family recipe and learn more about the rich history of moonshine in East Tennessee. Our authentic copper stills are always working to keep the shine flowing. Ole Smoky Moonshine offers 15 flavors for purchase in our bottle shop! Our distillery is open for self-guided tours to all ages so bring the whole family. Our holler has live bluegrass music playing every day from noon until dark. Come by, grab a jar, and sit a spell with us! Shine responsibly!

Samplings start at $5

Must be 21+ to taste. Must be 13+ to join.

Gatlinburg Space Needle

This 407-foot observation tower overlooks downtown Gatlinburg and provides a stunning 360-degree view of the Great Smoky Mountains that will have you coming back every season! Ride our glass elevators all the way up to the observation deck where you and your loved ones will enjoy a complimentary “Higher Learning” experience and free viewfinders that enhance the breathtaking scenery.!

Adults $15.95 Children $9.95 Seniors $12.95

Suitable for all ages
