Quick Facts

Gatlinburg was named after Radford Gatlin who happened to be the most hated man in town. Moonshine was legalized in Gatlinburg is 2009. The first log cabin built in Gatlinburg still stands today 220 years later. This beautiful city is the second most popular wedding destination in the US. Gatlinburg is home to the only ski resort in the entire state of Tennessee.
